Our fleet includes newer model Dodge Caravans and Chevy Starcraft buses. Our vehicles are inspected on a weekly basis for tire pressure and tread, all signal lighting, fluid levels, brake pads, and other general operational mechanisms in order to repair and maintain our fleet at the highest level. We utilize a certified licensed mechanic to address any vehicle repairs and issues. Our vehicles undergo Illinois Department of Transportation safety inspections every six months. Our maintenance records are stored in a computerized database system that assists us in tracking and monitoring our fleet. All of our vehicles are stored in a safe and secure lot.

  • Exclusively made up of newer model minivans and small buses, including wheelchair accessible vehicles.
  • Clearly identified with Kidztransport signage.
  • Maintained on a daily basis by a highly qualified maintenance staff.
  • Equipped with two-way radios, a GPS monitoring system, and audio/video recording devices with 30-day backup.
  • Kept in a clean, secure location.
  • Inspected and Certified by the Illinois Department of Transportation every six months.

Our operations are designed to offer school districts, parents and students a simple and efficient method to coordinate student transportation. School district transportation requests are received and processed by our dispatch department in a timely manner so that most requests can be met within one to two days. Our dispatchers contact parents to inform them of pick-up and drop-off times as well as introducing them to our company and answering any questions they may have. We have bilingual staff available to assist in communication with families if necessary. Our transportation staff is available from 5:00 a.m. until 5:30 p.m. (or when the last vehicle returns). We have an answering service available to take calls outside of these hours. Our routes are currently planned by our dispatch staff using routing technology to provide the most efficient and cost-effective routes. When possible, we coordinate routes between districts as a way to save money for participating districts.

  • Kidztransport Services averages 130,000 miles of student transportation a month and have never had a major accident with any injuries since opening in 2011.
  • Federal regulations allow the use of vans to transport students. Kidztransport Services’ vans meet the same strict safety requirements as school buses
  • Kidztransport Services’ drivers are specially trained and required to meet higher qualification, training, and testing standards as compared to other drivers on the road.
