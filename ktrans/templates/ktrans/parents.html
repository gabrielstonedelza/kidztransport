Parents


All Parents would be notified as soon as their kid is picked-up or dropped-off.

Transportaion pick-up process

1. When the driver pulls up to the student’s home, the driver will wait 3 minutes after the student’s assigned pick up time.
2. After a 3 minute wait, the driver will leave and continue on their route.
3. Attendance will be kept for all absences and “no shows” and shared with appropriate schools or internships.

Transportaion drop-off process

1. Students will only be dropped off at location designated by the district.

How to report an absence

  • If you know your child will not be needing transportation, please call the office at 773-349-0780 | 312-863-9262 to inform us of your child’s absence.
  • When you call, please tell us if your child will not need transportation in the AM, PM or all day.
